About Us

ICT Intrade is a leading merchandising, sourcing, procurement, and supply chain management company in India. We are a group of young professionals who specialize in home textiles, accessories, floor coverings, and apparel. We collaborate with leading retailers, importers, distributors, wholesalers, catalogue companies, and boutique businesses throughout North America and Europe.
ICT Intrade distinguishes itself by providing tailored sourcing solutions, earning the trust and appreciation of our partners.
Join ICT Intrade to experience innovation, dependability, customer satisfaction and long-term success.

Our Mission

At JYSP, our objective is to source products quickly, ethically, and intelligently while preserving or enhancing product quality in order to generate smiles. We aim for cost effectiveness to guarantee happiness in each and every transaction.

Our Vision

Our vision at ICT Intrade is to be the most trusted partner for sourcing excellence in the Indian Subcontinent, setting the standard for reliability and admiration.

Our Values

The three tenets of ICT Intrade's dedication are integrity, excellence, and customer-centricity. We establish a tapestry of principles that resonates with the fundamentals of your goals through integrating sustainability, innovation, and trust into every sourcing experience.
